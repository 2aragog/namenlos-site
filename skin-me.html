The Story
A whip with a bone handle. A girl named Rita in Berlin.

She should never have touched it.

Rita turns left when she meant to turn right. The alley narrows. The door has no sign. Inside, the shopkeeper is already waiting — as if he knew she'd come.

Behind the glass, a whip. Black leather, braided tight. The handle is bone — old, warm, humming. She reaches out. The shopkeeper says nothing. He doesn't need to. Some things choose you.

What follows is Berlin at 3 AM. Oranienstraße after midnight. A cezve of Turkish coffee that boils without fire. A man named Maks who knows things he shouldn't. And a force — dark, ancient, insatiable — that has lived inside the whip for centuries, waiting for skin it recognizes.

This is not a love story. This is a story about what love does when it has teeth.

⚠ Content Warning This book contains explicit sexual content, dark themes, psychological intensity, and scenes of emotional and physical violence. Intended for adult readers 18+. The dark force is the cursed whip, not the male lead.
